DAMLA Lab (Data Analysis and Machine Learning Application Research Laboratory)

The DAMLA Lab, located within Istanbul University's Department of Physics, focuses on research in data analysis and machine learning applications. Our work spans experimental and phenomenological high energy physics, CMS Experiment contributions, and interdisciplinary applications such as medical imaging. We aim to bridge cutting-edge research in physics with modern computational techniques.
